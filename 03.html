Athletics
It is a sport that contains a set of disciplines grouped into races, jumps, throws, combined tests and walking. It is the art of surpassing the performance of opponents in speed or endurance, distance or height

Basketball

Basketball
It is a sport that is played as a team on different surfaces, covered or not, and consists of scoring as many "baskets" or as many as possible in the time that the game lasts. Each team consists of five players who attack to score goals and defend when possession of the ball is the opposing team's

Talk at least 10 minutes a day

Talk at least 10 minutes a day
Walking burns more fat and calories than other exercises, helps the cardiovascular system activate and fortify, and helps you eliminate cholesterol harmful to the body. Walking increases resistance to exertion, to tiring less and later.

Swimming

Swimming
It is a sport that consists of moving from one place to another in the water without the person touching the ground. Swimming can be practiced in the pool or in open water

Soccer

Soccer
Game between two teams of eleven players each, whose objective is to enter the opposing goal a ball that can not be touched with the hands or arms, except by the goalkeeper in his goal area

Jogging 3 or 4 days a week

Jogging 3 or 4 days a week
It is one of the main tools to burn calories and get weight loss. Therefore, many dietitians recommend running a few minutes a day or starting running.
