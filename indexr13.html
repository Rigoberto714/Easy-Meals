Soy beans

PREPARATION TIME: 35 MIN

ESTIMATED BUDGED: $10

5 PORTIONS

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of soy protein
  • 1 pound of beans
  • 2 onions
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 oil tablespoons
  • Salt to taste

PREPARATION:

  • 1. Place the soybeans in a container and add 1 1/2 cups of boiling water. Let stand 15 minutes.
  • 2. Cook the beans with onion, salt and garlic.
  • 3. Cook the chili, onion and garlic and blend.
  • 4. Add the soy and finally add the beans.
  • 5. You can accompany with nachos or fried tortilla.
  • 6. Enjoy!

TIP

The soybean blend provides

a highly nutritious supplement.
