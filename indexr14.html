Soy ceviche

PREPARATION TIME: 35 MIN

ESTIMATED BUDGET: $10

8 PORTIONS

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of soy protein
  • 1 bunch of good herbs
  • 1 bunch of coriander
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Lemons and salt to taste

PREPARATION:

  • 1. Place the soybeans in a container and add 1 1/2 cups of boiling water. Let stand 15 minutes.
  • 2. Finally chop the vegetables and place in a container.
  • 3. Add the lemon juice to the soybeans and season with salt.
  • 4. Let stand and serve

TIP

Remember to only use natural herbs.
