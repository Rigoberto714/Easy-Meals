Break Fast

Breakfast is considered the most important meal of the day because the food eaten is the first after a long period of fasting derived from sleeping hours; breakfast also precedes the hours of the day in which the most activity is carried out.

Lunch

Lunch provides the energy the body needs to face any activity. Lunch is important to replenish energy, it gives a good dose of nutrients to your body. Lunch re-energizes your body, it can raise your blood sugar levels, so you can maintain your focus and concentration.

Dinner

Dinner should be light and not heavy. Our digestive system works when we sleep, that is why it is necessary that the dinner helps our body functions to develop correctly and to allow us a good rest.

Routines

Here we have some exercises for you

Yoga

Yoga is freedom. By exercising the discipline of Yoga, we help our body and mind to be healthy. Yoga also helps us to clear our mind and be relaxed, avoiding the stress of everyday life, and allows us to concentrate on our activities.

Running

Running helps to leave the stress of the day behind by releasing endorphins or happiness hormones. This activity increases our self-esteem. By practicing this sport you help your body to develop endurance and be in good physical condition.

Swimming

Swimming is a very good and interesting sport as it improves our memory and prevents stress. When you practice swimming you get respiratory benefits and your heart will be healthy. Swimming also helps keep your joints flexible and achieve greater endurance.

Interesting facts

The importance of water

We cannot live without water. Our body is composed of 50% to 70% water depending on the stage of our life. We need water to live as well as to help us fight disease. If you have not been drinking water, now is the time!

Fruit and vegetables

Consuming fruits and vegetables daily, helps us to have energy for the rest of the day, providing us with vitamins and necessary and natural supplements that our body needs. Fruits and vegetables provide vitamins A and C, which are essential for disease prevention.

Study

Studying, learning and good nutrition go hand in hand. Eating a good diet today will help you build a healthy and happy future, as well as improve your ability to study.
