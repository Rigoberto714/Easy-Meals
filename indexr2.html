CARROT BALLS

PREPARATION TIME: 30 MIN

ESTIMATED BUDGET: $10

5 PORTIONS

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1/4 cup soy
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 green pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • 2 tablespoons of rice
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • salt to taste
  • 1 garlic clove

PREPARATION:

  • 1. Wash the vegetables.
  • 2. Then chop onion, green pepper, and coriander.
  • 3. Grate the carrot.
  • 4. Moisturize the soybeans, seasoning with salt and garlic.
  • 5. Mix the soy meat with the previously chopped carrot, onion, cooked rice and green chili.
  • 6. Beat the eggs and add them to the mixture.
  • 7. Preheat a saucepan and add the oil.
  • 8. Form pancakes and fry.

TIP

Carrot contains a significant

amount of vitamin A and E.
